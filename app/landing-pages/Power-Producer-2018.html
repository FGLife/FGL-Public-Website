Punta Mita, Mexico

Power Producer Conference
May 6 - 10, 2018

Congratulations!

Fidelity & Guaranty Life congratulates you on your 2017 sales success!
In recognition of your achievement, you and a guest are invited to join our Power Producer Conference,
May 6 - 10 at the Four Seasons Resort Punta Mita, Mexico.

Please register by March 15th to confirm your place at the conference.

Click to Register

The Details

Overview

The invitation is for the qualifier and one adult guest. The invitation is non-transferable and there is not a cash equivalent should you be unable or choose not to attend.

Inclusions:
  • Economy airfare for the qualifier plus one adult guest, 18 years or older, between the qualifier’s home airport and Puerto Vallarta Airport
  • Roundtrip transfers between Puerto Vallarta Airport and the One & Only Palmilla
  • Four nights at the Four Seasons Resort Punta Mita including gratuities, resort fees, tax and service charges
  • Meals during hosted functions. Additional costs are at the guests own expense. Please see the agenda for a list of planned functions
  • Choice of activity on Wednesday, May 9
The following are not included and will not be covered by FGL:
  • Airline baggage fees
  • Passport fees
  • Airline upgrades including economy plus, more leg room and preferred seating options
  • Activity costs incurred for any additional guests beyond the one allowed adult guest,
    including any children
  • Transportation and parking on the ‘home’ side of your trip
  • Travel and medical insurance

Air Travel

FGL will cover economy airfare for the qualifier plus one adult guest, 18 years or older. Our travel agent, Direct Travel, will receive a copy of your completed registration and send a proposed itinerary for your review.

Passports are required for travel to Mexico. Due to international passport rules, US visitors to Mexico must have a passport that is valid for, at minimum, 6 months from the date of travel. If your passport expires 6 months or less from the date of travel a new passport must be obtained prior to your trip. At this time, please double check that your passport is valid and ready to go for May.

FGL will not cover the following:
  • Upgraded class of travel including economy plus, additional legroom or preferred seating.
  • Ticket charges beyond the qualifier and additional adult guest
  • Baggage fees
  • Passport fees
  • Lost immigration card charges (if incurred)
For questions regarding tickets, fare upgrades at your own, adding children or any other travel related concerns, please contact our partners at Direct Travel and reference that you are with the ‘Fidelity & Guaranty Life Power Producer Trip’:

Main Tel: (781) 326-7800, press 2 for the meetings department
Email: meetings_norwood@dt.com

Airport Transfers
FGL will cover airport transportation between Puerto Vallarta International Airport (PVR) and the hotel. Additional information will be included in your pre-trip package so that you can locate the transportation upon arrival. The resort is a 45-minute transfer from the airport.


Hotel
The Power Producer Conference invitation includes four nights at the Four Seasons Punta Mita including room rate, resort fee, tax and service. Incidentals are on own and a credit card will be required at check-in to cover any personal incidentals.

Pre or Post Stays:
If you wish to arrive early or extend your stay, please contact Molly O’Brien to confirm hotel availability. Our group rate at the hotel ranges from $535 per night plus applicable taxes and does not include food & beverage packages. If you would like to extend your stay, a credit card will be required in order to secure your additional nights.

Bringing Children:
If you plan to bring your children additional costs will apply and are at your expense.

The maximum occupancy for guest rooms is:
  • Three adults with an additional charge of $129 USD per night.
  • Two adults and two children. The children must be 18 years old or younger.
Should you not fit into one of the above two categories, a second room will be required at your cost and based on availability at the time of request.

Check In Time:
Check in time is at 3pm and check out time is at noon.

FAQs

Weather & Timezone
The average temperature in May is in the high 80s during the day. The evenings are warm and likely to reach the low 70’s. Punta Mita is located in the Central Standard Time Zone (CST).

Attire
The resort offers a casual and relaxed atmosphere. We do not require gentlemen to wear a jacket at any point in the agenda. Additional attire notes will be updated on this website as we get closer to the trip, as well as included in your pre-trip package.

These notes serve as a guide for those who are curious:
  • Sunday Welcome Reception: Casual attire, dinner will be served on the beach
  • Business Session: Both business sessions are business casual, jackets are not required
  • Monday Evening: We have selected local restaurants all with a casual atmosphere
  • Power Producer Recognition Dinner: This evening is our dressier of the trip. If men would like to wear a jacket, this would be the night. Ladies would be comfortable in casual style cocktail wear or sun dresses. Either way, lightweight clothing is encouraged
  • Farewell Dinner: We’ll dine poolside in a casual but festive environment. Resort casual is appropriate
Travel Documents:
Passports are required to travel to Mexico. At this time please take a moment to confirm that your passport is valid for your travel in May and that it is valid for at least 6 months following your travel dates. Please see the Travel section of this website for additional details.

Bringing Children:
Please keep in mind that our Power Producer Conference is a reward for outstanding production, and our program has been designed for qualifiers and their adult guests. However, we have made certain allowances within our program agenda for participation by children; please see further details below. All children under the age of 18 must be accompanied by an adult at all times.

  • Group Meals: Children may attend at no additional expense
  • Sunday, May 14: Welcome Reception & Dinner
  • Wednesday, May 9: Farewell Dinner
Children are not permitted at the dine-around evening or Recognition Awards Dinner. A kids club is available for children between the ages of 4-11.

Currency & ATM Access:
The hotel does not have an ATM on property. Please obtain any pesos that you would like to have at the airport or prior to travel. ATMs are available locally in the town of Punta Mita.

Gratuities:,
FGL has covered most all gratuities include driver, bellman, housekeeping. Any additional gratuities are at your own discretion and expense.

WiFi & Phone Access:,
The resort offers complimentary WiFi while on property. International access should be added to your individual phone plan should you wish to use your phone abroad.
