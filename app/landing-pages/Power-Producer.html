Punta Mita, Mexico

Power Producer Conference
April 28 - May 2, 2019

Congratulations!

F&G congratulates you on your 2018 sales success and invites you and a guest to join us April 28 through May 2 at the stunning Four Seasons Resort Punta Mita.

Please register by March 15th.

Click to Register

The Details

Overview

The invitation is for the qualifier and one adult guest, 18 years or older. It is non-transferable and there is not a cash equivalent should you be unable or choose not to attend.

Inclusions:
  • Four nights at the Four Seasons Resort Punta Mita including gratuities, resort fees, tax and service charges
  • Economy airfare for the qualifier plus one adult guest, 18 years or older, between the qualifier’s home airport and Puerto Vallarta Airport
  • Roundtrip transfers between Puerto Vallarta Airport and the resort
  • Meals during hosted functions. Additional costs are at the guests own expense. Please see the agenda for a list of planned functions
  • Choice of activity on Wednesday, May 1
The following are not included:
  • Airline baggage fees
  • Passport fees
  • Airline upgrades including economy plus, more leg room and preferred seating options
  • Activity costs incurred for any additional guests beyond the one allowed adult guest, 18 years or older
    including any children
  • Transportation and parking on the ‘home’ side of your trip
  • Travel and medical insurance

Air Travel

F&G will cover economy airfare for the qualifier plus one adult guest, 18 years or older. Our travel agent, Direct Travel, will receive a copy of your completed registration and send a proposed itinerary for your review.

Passports are required for travel to Mexico. Due to international passport rules, US visitors to Mexico must have a passport that is valid for, at minimum, 6 months from the date of travel. If your passport expires 6 months or less from the date of travel a new passport must be obtained prior to your trip. At this time, please double check that your passport is valid.

F&G will NOT cover the following:
  • Upgraded class of travel including economy plus, additional legroom or preferred seating
  • Ticket charges beyond the qualifier and additional adult guest
  • Baggage fees
  • Passport fees
  • Lost immigration card charges (if incurred)
For questions regarding tickets, fare upgrades at your own, adding children or any other travel related concerns, please contact our partners at Direct Travel and reference that you are with the ‘Fidelity & Guaranty Life Power Producer Trip’:

Main Tel: 781.326.7800, press 2 for the meetings department
Email: meetings_norwood@dt.com

Airport Transfers

F&G will cover airport transportation between Puerto Vallarta International Airport (PVR) and the hotel. Additional information will be included in your pre-trip package so that you can locate the transportation upon arrival. The resort is a 45-minute transfer from the airport.


Hotel
The Power Producer Conference invitation includes four nights at the Four Seasons Punta Mita including room rate, resort fee, tax and service. Incidentals are on own and a credit card will be required at check-in to cover any personal incidentals.

Pre or Post Stays:
If you wish to arrive early or extend your stay, please contact Direct Travel to confirm hotel availability. This can be done as part of your airfare request. Our group rate at the hotel ranges from $565 per night plus applicable taxes and does not include food & beverage packages. If you would like to extend your stay, a credit card will be required in order to secure your additional nights.

Bringing Children:
If you plan to bring your children additional costs will apply and are at your expense.

The maximum occupancy for guest rooms is:
  • Three adults with an additional charge of $129 USD per night.
  • Two adults and two children. The children must be 18 years old or younger.
Should you not fit into one of the above two categories, a second room will be required at your cost and based on availability at the time of request.

Check In Time:
Check in time is at 3pm and check out time is at noon.

It’s activity day! Select an activity and join F&G for some local fun. Below are our offerings along with some additional details. Please make your selection on the registration portion of the site.

Surfing Lessons
A short transfers into the town of Punta Mita, you will meet our professional guides. Guest will visit one of the local beaches which will be determine that day based on surf conditions. We plan to take guests by boat which is the most exciting way to approach your surfing destination. The activity will include three hours of private instruction. Please indicate your level on the registration.

Punta Sayulita Beach Club
Depart the resort by power boat to ‘Punta Sayulita’. Punta Sayulita is a private, hilltop club that F&G has reserved for the day. 360-degree views of the ocean, mountains and beaches await including an incredible culinary experience, live music and more. We will depart the resort at 9:30 AM and return at 2:00 PM.

Deep Sea Fishing
Enjoy a morning of fishing the waters of the Mexican Riviera and the famous Bandera Bay aboard a Cabo 35ft fishing yacht. Although prime fishing season is wrapping up in May, it is still a fisherman’s paradise. Expect to fish for Dorado, Blue and Black Striped Marlin and Sailfish. An early departure at approximately 6:30 am with a return to the hotel by 2:00 pm.

Combo Zipline & ATV Tour
Located a short drive from the hotel, Rancho Vista Paraiso offers some beautiful views of the Pacific, Marietas Islands and the unique peninsula of Punta Mita. The activity is a combination of a 2 rope zipline followed by a scenic tour through country roads, mountains, beach and the village of Higuera Blanca, all are mildly challenging. We will depart from the hotel at 8:30 am and return at 12:30 pm.

Golf at Pacifico
Get ready for the Nicklaus-designed course with either holds along the water. Enjoy the novelty and challenge of hold 3B, ‘the Tail of the Whale’, dubbed the world’s only natural-island green.

Shotgun start at 8am

The Spa at the Four Seasons
Choose from either a 60-minute massage or facial.

Cuevas Beach

Useful Details

Weather & Timezone
The average temperature in April & May is in the high 80s during the day. The evenings are warm and likely to reach the low 70’s. Punta Mita is located in the Central Standard Time Zone (CST).

Attire
The resort offers a casual and relaxed atmosphere. We do not require men to wear a jacket at any point in the agenda. Additional attire notes will be updated on this website as we get closer to the trip, as well as included in your pre-trip package.

These notes serve as a guide for those who are curious:
  • Sunday Welcome Reception: Casual attire, dinner will be hosted at a restaurant at the resort
  • Business Session: Both business sessions are business casual, jackets are not required
  • Monday Evening: We have selected local restaurants all with a casual atmosphere
  • Power Producer Recognition Dinner: This evening is our dressier of the trip. If men would like to wear a jacket, this would be the night. Ladies would be comfortable in casual style cocktail wear or sun dresses. Either way, lightweight clothing is encouraged. Ladies should consider their shoe choice as we will have dinner in the grass
  • Farewell Dinner: Dinner will be served on the beach
Travel Documents:
Passports are required to travel to Mexico. At this time please take a moment to confirm that your passport is valid for your travel in May and that it is valid for at least 6 months following your travel dates. Please see the Travel section of this website for additional details.

Bringing Children:
Please keep in mind that our Power Producer Conference is a reward for outstanding production, and our program has been designed for qualifiers and their adult guests. However, we have made certain allowances within our program agenda for participation by children; please see further details below. All children under the age of 18 must be accompanied by an adult at all times.

  • Group Meals: Children may attend at no additional expense
  • Sunday, April 28: Welcome Reception & Dinner
  • Wednesday, May 1: Farewell Dinner
Children are not permitted at the dine-around evening or Recognition Awards Dinner. A kids club is available for children between the ages of 4-11. Babysitting services are available through the concierge for a fee.

Currency & ATM Access:
The hotel has an ATM in the lobby.

Gratuities:,
F&G has covered most all gratuities including driver, bellman, housekeeping. Any additional gratuities are at your own discretion and expense.

WiFi & Phone Access:,
The resort offers complimentary WiFi while on property. International access should be added to your individual phone plan should you wish to use your phone abroad.
