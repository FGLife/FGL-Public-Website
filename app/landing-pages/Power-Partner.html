Power Partner 2018

PUNTA MITA

Please join Fidelity & Guaranty Life for our Power Partner Meeting.

Sunday, May 6 – Thursday, May 10, 2018
Four Seasons
Punta Mita, Mexico

Click to Register

  • Agenda

  • Useful Details

  • Optional Excursions

 

Hotel Rate & Incidentals

Room and tax will be covered by Fidelity & Guaranty Life. A credit card will be required at check-in to cover your personal incidentals. Should you wish to arrive early or extend your stay, please contact Molly O’Brien to make arrangements, molly.obrien@fglife.com or 443.240.3928

Check-In Time

Hotel check-in time is at 3:00 pm and checkout time is at noon.

Contact Information

Fairmont Pacific Rim
Main Tel: 1.604.695.5300
http://www.fairmont.com/pacific-rim-vancouver

Travel

Please include your air travel preferences and requirements in the registration. FGL is pleased to cover your airfare to Vancouver.

For planning purposes, our Welcome Reception is a cruise on the harbor beginning at 5:30 pm. We hope you can plan your arrival flights to join the event. On Wednesday our General Session will conclude before lunch and our activity is optional. Please plan your departure as needed, but we of course hope you can join our activity to Whistler!
 

Agenda

Monday, October 2

All-day arrivals

Pacific Yachts departing from Coal Harbor

*Individual meeting times will be communicated

Starlight Ballroom Foyer C

Life Breakout, Emerald Ballroom

Wednesday, October 4

7:30 am: Breakfast buffet
Starlight Ballroom

Starlight Ballroom C

Optional Departures following General Session

For guests who would like to stay we have an incredible afternoon activity planned for you!

Aerial Adventures! Guests will depart Vancouver by float plane from Coal Harbor Marina, located adjacent to the Fairmont, and enjoy a stunning 45-minute flight to Whistler. After landing on Green Lake, guests will be escorted to Zip Trek for a unique aerial adventure. Following the zip lines, float planes will return to Vancouver.

6:30 pm: Dinner at Cioppino’s

Thursday, October 5

Departures

Hotel check out time is at noon. Transfer time to the airport is approximately 30 minutes. For planning purposes, hotel departure time is 2 ½ hours prior to flight time.
 

Weather & Time Zone

In Vancouver in early October, temperatures range from the high 50’s during the day to the lower 50’s in the evening. The weather in the Vancouver area can be hard to predict. We suggest you come prepared for some cooler weather, a warm day and possibly a rain shower.

Attire

Business casual is suggested for all of the events.

Time Zone

Vancouver is in the Pacific Time Zone.
