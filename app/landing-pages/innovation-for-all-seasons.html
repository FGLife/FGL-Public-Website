FG Retirement Pro® Innovation for All Seasons

  1. Home
  2. Landing-Pages
  3. Innovation for All Seasons

Help turn clients’ visions of retirement into a reality by offering FG RETIREMENT PRO,® a modified single premium fixed indexed deferred annuity.

Help Maximize Savings

“Fees make a huge, dramatic impact on your total savings nest egg,” Laurie Rowley, President of non-profit National Association of Retirement Plan Participants says1.

FG Retirement Pro® charges no account fees or rider charges for the life of the contract. Surrender charges do apply for the first 12 years (state variations exist).

Help Protect the Retirement Plan

FG Retirement Pro® was specifically developed to keep retirement savings and income safe from market downturns while enhancing the opportunity for long-term growth potential.

With the FG Retirement Pro®, the Account Value earns a minimum fixed interest rate. It can’t earn 0%.

It may earn much more through Breakthrough, a feature that’s first of its kind. If Breakthrough is achieved, the Account Value has the same growth rate potential as the Benefit Base.

Living Longer

FG Retirement Pro’s® Guaranteed Minimum Withdrawal Benefit (GMWB) feature creates a lifetime income while maintaining flexibility until annuitization is required2.

FG Retirement Pro® focuses on growing income over time by crediting indexed interest directly on the GMWB feature’s Benefit Base as opposed to the annuity’s Account Value.

This innovation produces the opportunity for the income Benefit Base to grow faster. The Benefit Base is used for computing the GMWB and is not available in a lump sum.

Wealth Transfer

Sometimes plans don’t work out the way we intended. Preserving an increasing benefit for loved ones and family requires an innovation like the Enhanced Death Benefit which is paid in lieu of the Base Annuity Death Benefit.

There are no fees or charges for this unique legacy builder.
