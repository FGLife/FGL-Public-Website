Changing Your Contracting Status

In order to transfer policies to an agent already under agreement with Fidelity & Guaranty Life Insurance Company, please forward a request in the form of a letter, with all the relevant change information to Fidelity & Guaranty Life.

This request may be to transfer the servicing of policies.

Requests may be mailed to:
Agency Services - Commissions
c/o Fidelity & Guaranty Life Insurance Company, 6th Floor
1001 Fleet Street
Baltimore, MD 21202

Releasing Agents

Requests to transfer an agent from one hierarchy (network) to another are handled using the following guidelines:

  • Appointment date is greater than six (6) months and
  • No submitted business within the past six (6) months
A Vector check will be completed on all hierarchy change requests to ensure agent is in good standing within the industry. If we determine there is an outstanding Vector we will require proof the agent has worked out a payment plan with the carrier noted. If Vector is not resolved, we reserve the right to terminate the agent's Fidelity & Guaranty Life appointment providing 30-day notice. If there is an outstanding Fidelity & Guaranty Life agent debit balance, the debt must be paid in full prior to any transfer/hierarchy change.

Agents/Agencies with an outstanding debt are required to pay the balance in full prior to any consideration of a hierarchy release. We may permit the reduction of agent debt to be offset by submission of new business within the initial thirty days.

The agent needs to obtain an unconditional release from their current IMO; if an agent has never produced business they will be able to receive up to street level compensation. Any request for an above street level contract will require Home Office approval.

Agents requesting to be terminated are eligible for rehire within the previous IMO providing they disclose an adequate production commitment.

A confirmation notice of any release will be sent to the Agent and the IMO.

View the required Agent Transfer Request for additional provisional information.
