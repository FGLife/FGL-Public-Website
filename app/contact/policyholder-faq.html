Is my policy information available online?

Yes. You can view your policy and submit service requests by registering for our Policyholder Portal.

To log in for the first time, you must have:

  • An active policy number
  • An active email account
  • Social security number or tax identification number (TIN)
If you do not have your policy number, you can contact our Customer Service Department at 888.513.8797. Our customer care representatives are happy to walk you through the registration process.
How do I transfer funds between index options on my annuity contract?

Our annuity products allow you to reallocate funds on your annuity 30 days prior to or up to 25 days after your contract anniversary. A letter and reallocation form will be mailed to you 30 days prior to your anniversary. Should you decide to change your allocations for the upcoming contract year, please complete the form provided and mail or fax it to our service center
